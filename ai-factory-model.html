Cautionary Tale

Jellysmack

$1.7B valuation — SoftBank Vision Fund 2 led Series C

$1.7B → Collapse
  • 💀~300 of 500+ employees laid off across 2023–2024
  • 📉Facebook slashed CPM and video priority post-Reels pivot

  • 🔒Single-platform dependency on Facebook was the fatal mistake
  • 📊Negative unit economics: creators didn't recoup their advances
Key lesson: NEVER rely on one platform's CPM. Diversify revenue from day one. Advances to creators = death trap.

Platform Account Limits — What YouTube Actually Allows

YouTube
Up to 50 channels via Brand Accounts per Google Account. Safe zone: 10–20 channels with varied content, different niches. Risk: 50+ channels with similar content triggers spam detection.
TikTok
Business Account API: up to 5 accounts, 10 posts/day limit. One account per device (device ID binding). Phone verification required per account. Use TikTok Content Posting API for automation.
Instagram
Meta Business Suite: up to 25 accounts. Instagram Graph API: 25 API calls/hour, 200 posts/day per business account. Recommended: 5 accounts via Business Suite + API automation.

YouTube Tax: The W-8BEN-E Requirement

Without a W-8BEN-E form filed with Google, YouTube automatically withholds 24–30% of all US viewer revenue. File this form to claim treaty benefits. UAE (0%), Georgia (0%), UK (0%), Estonia (10%) are the best treaty jurisdictions for YouTube income.

AI Content IP — The Current Legal Reality

USA: pure AI output is NOT copyrightable. UK: human who "made arrangements" holds copyright. China: AI-generated image got copyright in 2023 Beijing court ruling. Practical reality: nobody challenges copyright on AI YouTube videos. Document your human creative input (prompts, editorial choices) regardless.

7 Critical Success Factors

What separates the 1% that succeed from the 99% earning under $100/mo (per Reddit community data)

01

Quality Over Quantity

5 channels with 50%+ retention beat 20 channels at 20%. YouTube's algorithm rewards watch time, not upload volume.

02

Human Curation is Non-Negotiable

100% automation = low quality = poor metrics = algorithm suppression. One human QA pass per video is the minimum viable standard.

03

Multi-Platform from Day One

YouTube + TikTok + Instagram as minimum. Never let one platform exceed 60% of revenue. This is exactly what killed Jellysmack.

04

Data-Driven Decisions

Target CTR: above 5%. Target AVD: above 40% of video length. Target RPM: above $3. Decisions come from analytics, not intuition.

05

Start NOW in Q1 2026

First-mover advantage is real and measurable. AI content market will saturate in 2–3 years. Channels with 2+ years of history have compounding SEO moats.

06

Brand Every Channel

Each channel needs identity, aesthetic, and a consistent voice — not just content volume. Branded channels survive algorithm changes far better.

07

Diversified Revenue Mix

AdSense + sponsorships + affiliate + B2B services. AdSense must be below 60% of total revenue by end of Year 1 to be safe.

+

Platform Compliance at Scale

Label AI content per TOS. Max 10–20 uploads/day. Unique content per channel. No coordinated behavior patterns. Safe limits protect the whole portfolio.
